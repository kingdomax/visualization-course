Questions to the dataset

Before you get started, take a look at the csv file under data/daily_climate_summary_no_missing_identical_first_entry.csv and think of three questions you would like to know from the data.

Please, formulate questions that can be answered by a visualization and do not ask the same kind of question twice. So if you asked "is there a relation between PRESSURE_AIR and TEMPERATURE_AIR?" then, do not ask the same question for another pair of attributes. Hint: Interesting things might be finding trends temperatures over the years, stations that stick out or group, or relations between seasons.
